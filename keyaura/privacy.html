Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

KeyAura is designed to respect your privacy. We collect only the minimum information necessary for the app to function:

1.1 Keyboard Usage Data

  • Keystroke counts: The number of times each key is pressed on your keyboard
  • Timestamps: When keystrokes occur (for daily data organization)
  • Keyboard layout: Your selected keyboard layout preference
  • App settings: Color scheme, heatmap style, and display preferences

1.2 System Information

  • Accessibility permissions: Whether you have granted accessibility permissions (required for keyboard monitoring)
  • License status: Whether you're using the free Core version or Pro version

2. Data Storage

2.1 Local Storage Only

All data collected by KeyAura is stored locally on your Mac. We do not upload, transmit, or store any data on our servers.

2.2 Storage Location

Your data is stored in:

  • Location: ~/Library/Application Support/KeyAura/
  • Format: SQLite database
  • Encryption: Data is stored in plain text (consider encrypting your Mac for additional security)

2.3 Data Retention

KeyAura retains your keyboard usage data indefinitely until you choose to delete it. You can delete all data at any time through the app's settings.

3. Data We DO NOT Collect

KeyAura does NOT collect:

  • ✗ The actual characters you type (we only count key presses)
  • ✗ Passwords, personal information, or sensitive data
  • ✗ Screenshots or screen recordings
  • ✗ Application usage or browsing history
  • ✗ Location data or device identifiers
  • ✗ Any data transmitted over the internet

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively for:

  • ✓ Generating keyboard heatmap visualizations
  • ✓ Displaying usage statistics (total keystrokes, most used keys)
  • ✓ Providing historical data analysis (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly)
  • ✓ Saving your app preferences
  • ✓ Managing your license status (free vs Pro)

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple App Store

If you download KeyAura from the Mac App Store, Apple may collect:

  • Your Apple ID and email address
  • Device information
  • App usage statistics

Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

5.2 Apple StoreKit (Pro Version)

If you purchase KeyAura Pro, Apple may collect:

  • Purchase transaction data
  • Payment information
  • Receipt verification data

Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

6. Data Sharing

KeyAura does not share your data with any third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all collected data through the app's interface
  • Delete: Permanently delete all keyboard usage data at any time
  • Export: Export your data as JSON for personal use
  • Opt-out: Disable keyboard monitoring at any time

8. Children's Privacy

KeyAura is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • ✓ All data stored locally on your device
  • ✓ No internet transmission of keystroke data
  • ✓ Regular app updates to address security vulnerabilities

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Notifying users through app updates (if applicable)

11. Accessibility Permissions

KeyAura requires Accessibility permissions to function. This permission allows the app to:

  • Monitor global keyboard events
  • Count key presses
  • Identify which keys are being pressed

Important: KeyAura does NOT read the actual characters you type. It only counts which keys are pressed and how often.
